Top 5 Mysterious Temples Of India

India is the land of 64 crore god and goddesses, with a multitude of holy cities and shrines of spiritual gurus to a motorcycle. Here, temples can be found at every step of the way, but only some of them are odd, strange, weird or unusual in one way or another. Some of these mysterious temples of India are famed because of their unconventional deities, some because of their exorcism rites, and some because they are more than 2000 years old.

Here is a list of eccentric temples of India that might blow your mind away. Take a look to see how many you have heard about.

  1. Mahendipur Balaji Temple, Rajasthan: Exorcise Your Demons
  2. Kamakhya Devi Temple, Assam: The Menstruating Goddess
  3. Devji Maharaj Mandir, MP:Venue For The Annual ‘Bhoot Mela’
  4. Kodungallur Bhagavathy Temple, Kerala:Abusing The Goddess
  5. Venkateshwar Temple, Andhra Pradesh:The Richest Temple
